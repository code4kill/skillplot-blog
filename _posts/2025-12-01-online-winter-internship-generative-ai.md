## Role & Learning Focus

Throughout this internship, you will:

- **Design reproducible AI workflows** using structured prompting techniques
- **Use AI as a learning, research, and thinking partner** to enhance your capabilities
- **Create content and artifacts** aligned with Skillplot's open content philosophy
- **Get guided exposure** to AI workflows for video, voice, and audio generation
- **Learn to critically evaluate** AI outputs, limitations, and responsible usage

## Expected Outcomes

Upon completing the internship, you'll have:

- **Reusable prompt templates and AI workflows** that you can apply to your own projects
- **AI-assisted documents, presentations, and research artifacts** demonstrating applied learning
- **Exploratory AI-generated media outputs** showcasing creative AI applications

*Note: Select high-quality artifacts may be showcased online. All internship learning material and outputs will be made publicly accessible later as part of Skillplot's open learning initiative.*

## Stipended Support

- **No monetary stipend**
- **One-month paid AI tool subscription reimbursement** (interns will subscribe independently and receive reimbursement as per guidelines)
